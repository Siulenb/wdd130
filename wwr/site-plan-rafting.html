Our Purpose.

Our purpose as team is to offer the best quality of experience on white water rafting with expert people on the field, that can help you to have amazing adventure with your friends or family.

Adventures audience

Our focus is to offer group of friends or families between 5 to 40 people adventures packages to enjoy an rafting in simple way that you do not need to worry on planning every details. We can offer your severals packages to build an amazing experience, we have three 10 types packages that will fit your need, these packages are divided by type of groups or families by ages adults 20+, and families that have minor from 14+ with their parents or guardian. These packges offer a place to stay, a level plan route (novice, medium, and pro), equipments, and food. We have done this since 2008 having customers happy of the amazing experience. Where you can feel relax by letting us to plan the best trip you will ever have.
